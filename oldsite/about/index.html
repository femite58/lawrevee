About Us

LAWREVEE INVESTMENT NETWORK is a leading investment company, international fund management and private equity company with interests in both developing regions and specific advanced markets. LAWREVEE INVESTMENT NETWORK uses a highly focused approach to investing and a hands-on operating philosophy in an effort to help grow and improve the profitability of each of its real estate companies. We seek to achieve well-defined goals with our partners by identifying and supporting growth opportunities and assisting with operational improvements. We focus on building solid relationships with proven management teams who share our commitment to integrity, professionalism, achievement, growth and success in business.

MISSION

To deploy global best models, and practices in delivering quality shelters to our clients in such a way that estate ownership and business becomes less stressful but more financially rewarding.

VISION

To be the top most internationally acclaimed estate company that delivers, blind-trust and unmatchable satisfaction to her clients.

VALUE

To make estate available for the average Nigerian with ease
